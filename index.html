Particle Simulator

Description

A WebGL project to simulate classical physics particles.

Inputs

  • #: The number of the particle
  • x1: The x-coordinate of the particle's initial position
  • x2: The y-coordinate of the particle's initial position
  • v1: The x-coordinate of the particle's initial velocity
  • v2: The y-coordinate of the particle's initial velocity
  • r: The radius of the particle
